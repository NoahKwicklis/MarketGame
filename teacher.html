Data & housekeeping

Hard clear deletes every trade, score, and player, ejects all connected students to the join screen, and restarts a fresh simulation under the same room code.

Average price of the k-th trade

Dot k averages the price of every trader’s k-th transaction that round (each trade counts once for the buyer’s sequence and once for the seller’s). Dashed line = theoretical equilibrium p* from current parameters.
